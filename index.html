The Chinese Internet has something special.

Statistically, China is the country with the largest population in the world. Witnessing the biggest economical growth in the history, it now forms the largest population of Netizens with about 700 Million people connected to the Internet. However, due to its network infrastructure, some services hosted outside of China can be really difficult to reach within the country..

After spending several years in China, we realized it was a recurrent problem. The fact is, most websites and services commonly used in the west are not fairly accessible to people living in China.
Conscious that a better situation is wished by everyone, we decided to start monitoring the most popular services used to build web applications, in order to help you making the most appropriate choices while building your product.
